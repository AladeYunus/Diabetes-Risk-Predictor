3. Model Limitations

The prediction model was trained on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset, which has known limitations:

  • The dataset includes only female participants of Pima Indian heritage aged 21+
  • Results may be less accurate for other demographics
  • The model achieves approximately 77% cross-validated accuracy — it will be wrong roughly 1 in 4 times
  • Missing or estimated input values reduce accuracy further
