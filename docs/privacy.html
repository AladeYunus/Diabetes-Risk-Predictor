Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 March 2026

DiabetesPredict UK is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health data.

All predictions run entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. The health metrics you enter never leave your device — no data is sent to any server.

2. Cookies

We use only one essential cookie:

  • cookie_consent — stored in your browser's localStorage to remember whether you accepted or rejected our cookie notice. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

3. Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type) as part of their hosting service. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Since we do not collect personal data, most data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, etc.) do not apply. However, you retain all rights under UK GDPR, including:

  • The right to be informed about data processing
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

5. Children

This tool is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
